"Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard for car sales analysis, utilizing advanced DAX functions to create year-to-date (YTD) and month-to-date (MTD) insights, KPIs, and sales trends across regions, companies, and car models. The project included dynamic filtering options, detailed visualization of sales by body style, color, and dealer region, and advanced forecasting techniques to predict future performance. Enhanced decision-making by integrating key metrics such as average price, total sales, and cars sold, while ensuring a user-friendly, interactive experience."
